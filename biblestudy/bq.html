Self-Introduction:

Hi. I’m Junhao Wang. You can call me Chuck. I’m a first-year graduate student at USC. I study computer science. Previously, I had an internship experience in a startup in Hong Kong. I developed a prototype application about online shopping. Basically, I was in charge of implementation on user interface and communication with the backend server. After that, I found that I enjoyed making products and reading feedbacks from users. So, I started working on two projects later.

The first one is a campus app. When I was a sophomore, my best friend came to me and said: Hey Junhao. Do you wanna make an app for our college? And I said yes, why not? So, we spent two months in coding from scratch and released the first version in 2015. In later 13 versions, we added 15 features. Since we had more and more users, we invited students in business, art, and journalism to our team. Before graduation, we also recruited 20 freshmen and taught them how to follow up.

Another project is about test preparation. It was a course project in software engineering. Because I thought it was a very great idea, my teammates and I made it into a real-world application. We designed some interesting features such as online leaderboards, daily challenge, and so on, to enhance learning experience. As usual, I made the iOS version which is available on App Store with almost 4,500 users.

So, these are my two major projects in my college life.
